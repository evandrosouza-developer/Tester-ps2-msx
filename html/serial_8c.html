◆ last_dma_tx_set_number_of_data

uint16_t last_dma_tx_set_number_of_data

Number of bytes requested to transmit by DMA (Global variable)

As there is no dedicated dma tx buffer, dma process shares uart_tx_ring_buffer with other processes that write on it. It brings information of how many bytes dma has just sent to be used on ISR_DMA_CH_USART_TX to update uart_tx_ring.get_ptr only after DMA process is finished. So, to clear room for new writings only after dma is concluded, it assures no loss of information and optimize uart_tx_ring_buffer usage.
