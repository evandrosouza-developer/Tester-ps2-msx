This library supports the ARM System Timer in the STM32F4 and STM32F1 series of ARM Cortex Microcontrollers by ST Microelectronics. In Tester-PS2_MSX, it is responsible to manage the timming and generate the scan pattern and starts the delay of the X line readings.
